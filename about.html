insight

A legacy spanning almost 90 years, Prime Chemical & Scientific Co. has been a trusted name in the world of scientific goods and laboratory chemicals.

Established in 1932, we have been dedicated to delivering complete laboratory solutions to our clients, backed by unparalleled support. Our expertise lies in supplying laboratory chemicals and scientific goods that meet the highest global standards, ensuring that our clients are equipped with the best tools for their research and experimentation. As distributors of major international brands such as Merck, Sigma, Honeywell, and Thermo Fisher, we take pride in offering a diverse range of products that cater to the needs of our clients across various industries.

At Prime Chemical & Scientific Co., we believe in offering value beyond the products we supply. Our team of experts is committed to providing dedicated support to our clients, helping them navigate through the complex world of laboratory solutions. We understand the critical role that scientific research plays in shaping the future, and we are honored to contribute to this noble pursuit by providing the best-in-class products and services.

Mission

At Prime Chemical & Scientific Co., we envision a future where scientific and laboratory goods are readily available to everyone, regardless of their location or industry. Our mission is to be the leading supplier of high-quality and innovative products that enable researchers, scientists, and technicians to make groundbreaking discoveries and advancements in their fields. We strive to continuously expand our product offerings and services to meet the growing and evolving needs of our customers. Our commitment to providing exceptional customer service and technical support sets us apart, and we will continue to raise the bar in the industry. As we look to the future, we are excited to embrace new technologies and advancements in science to push the boundaries of what is possible.

Vision

At Prime Chemical & Scientific Co., our mission is to provide our customers with the highest quality laboratory goods and chemicals to help facilitate their research and experiments. We strive to be a reliable and trusted supplier by offering exceptional customer service, competitive pricing, and timely delivery. From innovative products to a vast selection, we aim to provide solutions that meet the needs of our customers. We are committed to exceeding expectations and maintaining the highest standards of integrity, ethics, and safety in everything we do.

We blend the features of quality in every activity we carry out. We believe that the concept of quality must become a culture and not just a set of guidelines. The quality policy of the Company aims at achieving and exceeding expectations of its Customers & Vendors by adherence to quality systems encompassing every activity and involving every employee to ensure consistent delivery of the best quality services.
