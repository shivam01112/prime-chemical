Prime Chemical & Scientific embraces its position as one of the oldest name in the industry of Laboratory equipment and chemical supply in India. In 1932 as Dhingra Scientific, and later in 1987 as Prime Chemical & Scientific has always had one and only purpose: To provide high-quality laboratories essentials to the concerned industries. We have always imbibed the spirit of ethics, honesty, and commitment to our customers. Our complete and dedicated effort to our customers along with our principal portfolio of the most reputable manufacturers has made us a top name in this industry. We have a wholesome approach to provide consultation, sourcing and execution of the solution to chemical laboratories, microbiology laboratories, pharma-biotech manufacturing facilities, research institutes, food manufacturing facilities, automobile industries and many more.
